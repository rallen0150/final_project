About the Creater of Chowster

Thank you for visiting my site Chowster.co! This started out as a project for The Iron Yard, but this has turned out to be "my baby". Now about me. My name is Robbie Allen and I created this site for people to see which food truck is in your area based off your location. I am from Greenville, South Carolina so the site is centered around downtown Greenville. I went to USC-Columbia for Computer Science and Greenville Technical College for Electrical Engineering before going to The Iron Yard. I was apart of the Back-End Engineer classes of The Iron Yard. I have had very basic HTML before The Iron Yard, but not as much as I learned here. I previously built a page many years ago, but unfortunatly it is no longer on the internet. That class is what made me want to develop webpages for a living. I searched coding schools and I landed on the Iron Yard since there was a campus listed in Greenville. Being a Back-End Engineer, I deal with data that stores information across the web page. I am going to continue working on this page, but the goal is to make this page a mobile app for easier access.
